China's New Low-Cost Open-Source AI Model Outshines DeepSeek: A Game-Changer in AI Technology and Digital Transformation

Discover how China's new low-cost open-source AI model GLM-4.5 outshines DeepSeek in efficiency and capability. Learn about machine learning, deep learning, and digital transformation in AI technology.

In 2025, China has taken a significant leap forward in artificial intelligence with the introduction of a new low-cost, open-source AI model that surpasses its predecessor, DeepSeek, in efficiency and capability. This breakthrough reflects China's rising influence in the global AI landscape and marks a notable milestone in the ongoing digital transformation and evolution of emerging technology.

The Rise of China's Open-Source AI Ecosystem

Unlike many proprietary AI systems, China's open-source models—including DeepSeek and the latest release by companies like Z.ai (formerly Zhipu)—offer developers unrestricted access to the underlying code, enabling extensive customization and collaborative improvement. Notably, the new model, GLM-4.5, requires fewer computational resources than DeepSeek, cutting costs dramatically while enhancing task accuracy through advanced machine learning techniques and an "agentic" approach that autonomously decomposes tasks for better execution.

Key Advantages of GLM-4.5

  • Reduced Computational Requirements: Requires fewer resources than DeepSeek
  • Cost Efficiency: Dramatically cuts operational costs
  • Enhanced Task Accuracy: Improved performance through advanced machine learning
  • Agentic Approach: Autonomous task decomposition for better execution
  • Open-Source Access: Unrestricted code access for customization
  • Collaborative Development: Community-driven improvements and innovations

China's Open-Source AI Culture and Innovation

This efficiency gain is a direct result of China's strong open-source AI culture, which fosters faster innovation compared to the more closed, commercial AI development models prevalent in the West. The model GLM-4.5 is about half the size of DeepSeek's architecture and operates efficiently on eight Nvidia H20 chips, optimized for the Chinese market. This competitive edge is also reflected in the popularity of Chinese models like Moonshot AI's K2, which topped global rankings for open-source AI by delivering natural, engaging responses in natural language processing tasks.

Technical Specifications

  • Architecture Size: Approximately half the size of DeepSeek's architecture

  • Market Optimization: Specifically designed for the Chinese market
  • Performance Ranking: Topped global rankings for open-source AI
  • Response Quality: Natural and engaging responses in NLP tasks
